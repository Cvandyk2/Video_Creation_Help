Autobot

Manage and schedule short-form content across platforms, then push to native schedulers.

What it does

  • Calendar-based scheduling for YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Threads
  • Upload once, schedule to multiple platforms
  • Preflight checks for duration/size/aspect ratios
  • Push to native schedulers (where supported by platform APIs)

Security & Privacy

  • Uses official platform APIs with OAuth
  • Tokens are stored locally and can be revoked
  • Your media remains under your control
