Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Autobot handles information.

Data We Process

  • Media files and post metadata you upload to schedule.
  • Access tokens granted via platform OAuth to upload and schedule content.

How We Use Data

We use tokens solely to perform actions you request (e.g., upload videos, create scheduled posts). We do not sell personal data.

Storage & Retention

Tokens may be stored locally to keep your session active; you can revoke them via the platform at any time. Media is stored locally for scheduling and can be deleted by you.

Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except as required by law or as necessary to interact with the selected platforms’ APIs.

Your Rights

You can disconnect accounts or delete scheduled content at any time. For data requests, contact the app maintainer.
